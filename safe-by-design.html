Safe by Design

Haven isn't just hoping people are nice. Safety is built into every layer — from the moment you sign up to every message you send. So you can open up without worrying about what's on the other side.

How we keep Haven safe

📸

Selfie verification on signup

Every new account takes a live selfie. Screenshots are instantly rejected. AI-generated photos are detected and banned. If your face doesn't match your age group, you'll need to verify with a real ID.

🤖

Real-time AI monitoring

Every conversation is watched by AI 24/7. It's not reading to judge you — it's there to catch anything dangerous before it causes harm. Crisis language, abuse, grooming — it flags it all immediately.

🚨

Crisis detection

If someone mentions suicide, self-harm, or abuse, the AI steps in right away — not to lecture, but to make sure that person gets pointed toward real help. Haven takes this seriously.

🚫

Troll timeout system

Trolls and bad actors don't last long. The system escalates automatically — every offense gets a longer break, until they're gone for good.

Why this makes you feel calmer

When you know a space is genuinely safe, you can actually relax and be honest. You don't have to hold back or brace for someone to say something awful. That peace of mind is part of what makes Haven work — safety isn't just a feature, it's what makes real conversations possible.
