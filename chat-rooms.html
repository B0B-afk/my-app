Age-Group Chat Rooms

No more feeling like the only person going through it. Haven puts you in a room with people in the exact same stage of life — people who actually get what you're dealing with.

Why age groups matter

A 14-year-old stressed about exams and a 40-year-old stressed about work are going through very different things. Mixing them together doesn't help either one. Haven splits people into age groups so every conversation feels relevant — not random. You're not just talking to "anyone." You're talking to someone who gets your world.

Your rooms

Each age group has its own space. You can jump into the general room, find smaller group chats, or connect one-on-one with someone you vibe with. The conversation stays in your lane — your struggles, your season of life, your people.

How it keeps you calm

When you're stressed, the last thing you want is to explain your whole situation to someone who doesn't get it. In Haven's age rooms, context is already shared. You walk in and people already understand the kind of pressure you're under. That alone takes weight off. You can just talk.
