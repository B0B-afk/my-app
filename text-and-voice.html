Feature

Text & Voice

Some things are easier to type. Some things need to be said out loud. Haven gives you both — so you can communicate in whatever way feels right in the moment.

Your way, your pace

Not everyone processes things the same way. Some people need to carefully write out what they're feeling. Others just need to talk it through out loud. Haven doesn't force you into one mode — you choose what works for you, every time.

💬

Text chat

Type it out at your own pace. Think before you send. Edit your words. Text is perfect when you need to organize your thoughts or just aren't ready to speak.

🎙️

Voice calls

Sometimes you just need to hear a human voice. Jump into a voice room and talk — no cameras, no pressure, just voices. It feels closer to a real conversation.

Why no video?

🚫 No video — ever.

Video calls add pressure. You worry about how you look, your background, your face when you're emotional. That pressure gets in the way of being honest. Haven removed video entirely so you can focus on what actually matters — the conversation. No cameras means no performance. Just people being real.

Private or group — you decide

Jump into a group room with your age group and talk to everyone, or find someone you connect with and go private. You can create a small group chat with people you trust, or just hang in the open room. Haven adapts to however social you're feeling that day — and that's okay.

Say it however feels right.

Text. Voice. Group. Private. Haven works your way.
